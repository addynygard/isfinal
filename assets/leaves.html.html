This is your one stop, how-to guide complete with pictures, videos, and written steps so you will be prepared if someone invties you to go mountain biking!

Step by Step Guide on How To Start Mountain Biking:

  1. Aquire the right clothes and bike
    2. My bike is a YT, and here are some links to the best clothes for mountain biking!
    MTB Shirts and Shorts
  2. Find some friends who also like to mountain bike so you can have riding buddies!
  3. Pack up the bikes and drive or ride to a sick local trail in a 4Runner or Tacoma!
    4. Click here to find local trails with reviews!
  4. Start tracking your ride on Strava, because everyone knows that you Strava or it didn't happen obviously.
  5. Breathe super hard as you go uphill, and don't forget to complain about the brutal uphill but be sure to follow it up with how you are just "earning the downhill"!
  6. Send all the big drops, hit the side berms, yip and holler to your heart's extent, and chase your friends down the mountain!
  7. Take lots of sick pictures and videos!
  8. Show off your post-ride wheelies, track stands, and just ride in circles around the parking lot so your Strava says you rode longer than your buddies!
  9. Stop the recording on Strava!
  10. Hold your stomach and don't stop saying how hungrey you are
  11. a BIG burger with some fries, treat yourself to a choclate milkshake, or devour a huge stack of pancakes!
